Nail Gel Polish

Gel nails refer to a type of nail polish that is cured with a UV nail lamp to create a shiny, long-lasting manicure that doesn't require extensive time to dry. Gel nail polish usually follows a three-step process, which consists of a base coat, gel nail polish and finally, top coat.
